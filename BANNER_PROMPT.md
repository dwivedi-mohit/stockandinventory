# Invictus — Banner Image Prompt

## Concept
A refined, elegant brand hero banner for **Invictus** — an inventory management system by **BEE tech**. The design must look hand-crafted, printed on premium paper, not AI-generated. Minimalist, typography-driven, with subtle texture and warmth.

## Aspect Ratio
2800 × 1100 px (≈ 2.55:1)

## Layout (top to bottom)

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                      │
│          INVICTUS                    │  ← large serif bold, centered, drop shadow
│          ───── ◆ ─────              │  ← thin ornamental line + diamond
│      🐝 BEE   tech                   │  ← icon left, bold + light weight text
│   Stock & Inventory Management       │  ← light serif tagline
│                                      │
│                                v1.0  │  ← small sans-serif, bottom right
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

All elements are vertically centered as a single group. The composition should breathe with generous negative space.

## Elements

### 1. Background & Texture
- Warm off-white paper tone (#F7F6F4)
- Very subtle noise/grain texture (barely perceptible, 3% opacity)
- Soft vignette darkening toward edges (subtle, natural falloff)
- 1px thin rounded border in muted gray, inset 22px from edges

### 2. "INVICTUS"
- Font: Serif (e.g. DejaVu Serif Bold or similar)
- Size: Large, dominant (equivalent to ~180pt)
- Color: Very dark navy (RGB 26, 26, 46) with slight gradient — edges slightly lighter than center
- Subtle drop shadow, offset right-down by ~5px, blur radius ~8px, low opacity
- Letter-spacing: slightly tracked out (approx 8px between letters)
- Very subtle random vertical jitter per letter (< 1px) for hand-crafted feel

### 3. Ornamental Divider
- Thin horizontal line, centered, in muted gray (#A5A3AA)
- Two segments: left (approx 320px) and right (approx 320px) with a gap
- Small filled diamond (◆) at center in same color
- Positioned between INVICTUS and BEE tech

### 4. Bee Icon + "BEE tech"
- Icon: Small geometric bee silhouette/shape to the left of the text
  - Two translucent oval wings (top-right wing, bottom-left wing arrangement)
  - Oval body in dark gray (#373546) with lighter horizontal stripe details
- "BEE": Bold serif, matching INVICTUS style but smaller, color #373546
- "tech": Regular weight serif, lighter color (#878591)
- "tech" baseline-aligned with "BEE" (sits slightly lower to match baseline)
- Gap between icon and text, and between "BEE" and "tech"

### 5. Tagline
- Text: "Stock & Inventory Management"
- Font: Small serif (~42pt), light weight, color #AFADB4 (muted)
- Centered below BEE tech with generous spacing

### 6. Version
- Text: "v1.0"
- Font: Small sans-serif (~28pt), color #B9B9BE
- Positioned in bottom-right corner with padding
